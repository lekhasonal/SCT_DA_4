# Marketing Campaign Business Insights Report (EDA)

## Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Marketing Campaign Dataset to understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and purchase channel preferences. The goal is to generate actionable business insights and recommend strategies to improve marketing ROI.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Compare different purchase channels.
- Discover relationships between customer attributes and spending.
- Provide business recommendations based on insights.

## Dataset Information
- Total Records: 2,240 Customers
- Features: 29 Columns
- Data includes customer demographics, income, product spending, purchase history, and campaign responses.

## Data Cleaning
- Handled missing values.
- Checked and removed duplicate records.
- Converted date columns to appropriate formats.
- Created a Total Spending feature for analysis.

## Key Findings
- Wine Products generated the highest revenue.
- Meat Products were the second-highest revenue contributors.
- Store Purchases were the most preferred purchase channel.
- Higher-income customers tended to spend more.
- Opportunities exist to improve website conversion rates.

## Visualizations
- Correlation Heatmap
- Product Category Spending Analysis
- Purchase Channel Comparison
- Customer Spending Analysis

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on Wine and Meat Products.
- Target high-income customer segments.
- Strengthen in-store marketing strategies.
- Improve website user experience and conversion rates.
- Implement customer retention and loyalty programs.

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

## Conclusion
The analysis provided valuable insights into customer purchasing behavior and product performance. By focusing on high-performing products, optimizing marketing channels, and targeting valuable customer segments, businesses can improve marketing effectiveness and maximize ROI.
